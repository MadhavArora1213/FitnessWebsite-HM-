Best Exercise For Begineers

Bridge

The bridge will boost your booty all over.It works your gluteus maximum,medius, and minimus muscles.It'll also help get your heart rate up before you move on to more challenging exercises.

How to do it: Lie flat on your back. Bend your knees abd plant feet flat on the floor. Extend arms at your sides and press palms into the floor. Push your feet into the fllor and raise your hips until your body forms a straight line from knees to shoulders. Squeeze glutes and hold for a few seconds. Return to the starting position. Do 3 sets of 10 reps.

Knee push-up

This is a great way to build strength and stamina as you work your way up to classic push-ups.It target your pecs,cors,shoulders, and arms on to more challenging exercises.

How to do it: Start on hands and knees, keeping spine aligned and hands shoulder-width apart. Bend elbows to a 45-degree angle as you lower your body toward the floor. Push your palms into the floor to raise yourself back up. Do 3 sets of 8-10 reps.
